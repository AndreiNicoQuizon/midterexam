Mount banahaw a place with a beautiful mountains and beach

by Andrei Nico Quizon

The Mt. Banahaw is a former volcano on the island of Luzon in the Philippines. It has three peaks in the middle of the provinces of Laguna and Quezon. It is the highest mountain in the CALABARZON region. It is said to be a sacred mountain and is a popular destination among religious and pilgrims. Banahaw is a national park and protected area in the Philippines since 1941. It is now called Mt. Banahaw-Cristobal Protected Landscape covering 10,901 hectares of land. Banahaw last erupted in 1730. It destroyed the town of Sariaya at the foot of the mountain.
